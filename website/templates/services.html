Our Services

How can we enhance your safety, every step of the way?

Fire Alarm & Detection Systems

Addressable systems offer precise alarm location identification, enhancing response efficiency. Conventional systems provide reliable, zone-based alerts for straightforward fire detection.

Fire Suppression Systems

Our fire suppression systems include FM200 and NOVEC1230 for clean agent solutions, AUTO CO2 for quick and effective suppression, and FOAM systems for tackling flammable liquid fires.

Fire Fighting Systems

We offer a range of fire fighting systems including fire extinguishers for immediate response, fire hydrants for extensive water supply, and fire hose reels for efficient fire control.

Outsourcings

We provide comprehensive outsourcing services, offering a reliable supply of personal protective equipment (PPEs) and medical equipment to ensure safety and readiness in any situation.

HD Plumbing

Our HD Plumbing services offer advanced piping solutions, ensuring efficient and reliable water supply systems tailored for industrial and commercial applications.

Safety Training

We offer comprehensive safety training programs to educate and prepare your team for emergencies, ensuring everyone knows how to respond effectively.

Welding Fabrications

Our welding fabrications provide custom, high-quality solutions for all your structural and repair needs, ensuring durability and precision in every project.
